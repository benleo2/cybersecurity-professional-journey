# Port States & Service Detection

## What it is
Port states describe how a system responds to network probes.

## Why it matters in security
Port states reveal exposure and firewall behavior.

## Port States Observed
- Open
- Closed
- Filtered

## Commands Used
- nmap
- nmap -sV

## Hands-on Practice
- Identified open ports
- Observed service versions
- Analyzed exposure

## Defender Perspective
Only required services should be exposed and monitored.
