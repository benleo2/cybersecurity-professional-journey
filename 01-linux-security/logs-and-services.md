# Linux Logs & Service Security

## What it is
Linux logs record authentication, system events, and service activity.

## Why it matters in security
Logs are essential for detecting attacks and investigating incidents.

## Important Log Files
- auth.log
- syslog
- kern.log

## Commands Used
- cat
- tail
- grep
- lastb
- systemctl

## Hands-on Practice
- Viewed authentication logs
- Identified sudo activity
- Checked failed login attempts
- Monitored service status

## How attackers abuse this
Attackers may disable logging or hide services.

## How to defend
- Monitor logs regularly
- Limit services
- Enable alerts
